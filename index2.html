Simon and Garfunkel

“I did have a lucky thing going on there in my throat.” -Art Garfunkel

Simon and Garfunkel are an iconic folk rock duo. They were one of the most popular music acts of the 1960s.

The duo is comprised of:

  • Paul Simon
  • Art Garfunkel
